\section{Origin}
\noindent Original game: 東方永夜抄　～　Imperishable Night \\
Original title: 月まで届け、不死の煙　~　Reach for the Moon, Immortal Smoke \\
Theme: Extra Stage BOSS --- Fujiwara no Mokou's (藤原　妹紅) theme

\section{Summary}
The continuation of the previous song あさきゆめみし
Sung by Fujiwara no Mokou

\subsection{Verse \#1}
\furigana{怒}{いか}りは\furigana{永}{なが}い\furigana{年月}{としつき}に\furigana{晒}{さら}されて\furigana{熱}{ねつ}を\furigana{失}{うしな}う \\

\furigana{憎}{にく}しみは\furigana{深}{ふか}い\furigana{悲}{かな}しみに\furigana{覆}{おお}われて\furigana{枯}{か}れていった \\
Hatred was whithering away and covered in deep sadness

いずれなにもかも　\furigana{消}{き}え\furigana{失}{お}せる \\
Eventually everything will perish

\furigana{残}{のこ}るのは　私と\furigana{月}{つき}の\furigana{涙}{なみだ}\furigana{雫}{しずく}、 \\
All that's left behind is me and the moon's teardrops \tlnotelink{3}
