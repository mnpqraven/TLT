Theme: Stage 6 BOSS --- Sukuna Shinmyoumaru's (少名　針妙丸) theme

\section{Summary}
This is the second part to a two-part song, first one being 完全超悪アマノジャク. The song is sung from \href{https://en.touhouwiki.net/wiki/Shinmyoumaru_Sukuna}{Sukuna's} perspective, an \href{https://en.touhouwiki.net/wiki/Inchling}{Inchling} princess of loyal descendant who is tricked and deceived by \href{https://en.touhouwiki.net/wiki/Seija_Kijin}{Seija Kijin} to use the \href{https://en.touhouwiki.net/wiki/Miracle_Mallet}{Miracle Mallet} for her own benefit.
